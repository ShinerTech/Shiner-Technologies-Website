Why Choose Starlink?

Starlink is the world's first and largest satellite constellation using a low Earth orbit to deliver broadband internet capable of supporting streaming, online gaming, video calls and more.

It is ideally suited for rural and remote areas where internet connectivity has been unreliable or completely unavailable. With our professional installation services, you can ensure your hardware is mounted securely and optimally positioned to receive the best possible signal, no matter the weather.

Our Installation Process

  • Site Survey: We find the optimal location with a clear view of the sky to prevent obstructions.
  • Secure Mounting: We use high-quality mounts suitable for roofs, poles, or siding to ensure your dish is stable in extreme weather.
  • Cable Routing: Professional, clean cable runs from the dish into your home or business, completely weather-sealed.
  • Network Setup: Configuration of your Starlink router and integration with your existing network, including mesh systems.
