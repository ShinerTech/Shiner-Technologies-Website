About the Owner

Chris Shiner is a proud graduate of Manistique High School and a U.S. Army veteran. He served six years at Fort Bragg, North Carolina, as an Airborne Military Police Officer, completing an impressive 35 parachute jumps during his service. After earning his Associate’s Degree, Chris transitioned into the private sector, bringing with him valuable hands-on experience in wireless networking and security camera installations.

Chris is married to his wife Kayla, who is also a proud U.S. Army veteran. Together, they bring a shared commitment to discipline, integrity, and service. They are the proud parents of four children, and their strong family values are reflected in the way they run their business — with reliability, trust, and a focus on serving the community with excellence.
