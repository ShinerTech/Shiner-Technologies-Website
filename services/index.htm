Services

The IT professionals at Shiner Technologies have been supporting business technical systems for more than 30 years.

Your business relies on technical systems every day and Shiner Technologies has the experienced technicians to help keep you up and running. Shiner Technologies technicians have degrees in computer-related fields and industry certifications from Microsoft, Cisco, and CompTIA. Your systems are too important to trust to anyone else.

Video Surveillance

Protect your assets with Uniview high-definition surveillance. We install professional IP cameras with advanced night vision and remote access.

Monitor your property in real-time from anywhere in the world with secure smartphone integration.

Point to Point Connectivity

Expand your network across buildings or long distances without the need for expensive trenching. Our high-speed Point-to-Point wireless solutions provide reliable, enterprise-class connectivity where you need it most.

Ensure your remote locations stay connected with the same high performance as your main office.

Telephone Systems

Modernize your communications with 3CX and FortiVoice. We offer VOIP solutions that include mobile integration and auto-attendants.

Reliable, crystal-clear business communication whether you are in the office or on the go.

Scalable, Always-up-to-date solutions that give your business a competitive advantage.
